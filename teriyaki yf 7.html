Teriyaki Tuna Yellowfin

Teriyaki Tuna Yellowfin offers succulent ahi tuna steaks coated in a flavorful teriyaki glaze, paired with a spicy and creamy volcano sauce. Garnished with fresh scallions, this dish delivers a harmonious blend of savory and spicy flavors.

Ingredients

Teriyaki Tuna:

  • ⅜ cup teriyaki sauce
  • 3 ahi tuna steaks
  • 2 ¼ tablespoons white sesame seeds
  • 2 ¼ tablespoons black sesame seeds
  • 2 ¼ tablespoons vegetable oil

Volcano Sauce:

  • 4/7 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 ½ tablespoons chili garlic sauce
  • 1 ½ tablespoons sweetened condensed milk
  • ¾ teaspoon sugar

Garnish:

  • ¾ bunch scallions, sliced diagonally

Instructions

  1. For volcano sauce, combine mayonnaise, chili garlic sauce, sweetened condensed milk, and sugar in a small food processor or with an immersion blender. Adjust the heat level by adding chili garlic sauce slowly to reach desired spiciness. Set aside.
  2. Combine white and black sesame seeds in a shallow bowl. Dredge both sides of the tuna steaks in sesame seeds. Set aside.
  3. Heat vegetable oil in a large cast iron skillet or frying pan over high heat.
  4. When the oil is hot, place tuna steaks in the pan and cover. Cook for 2-3 minutes on each side, depending on desired doneness. Remove from pan.
  5. Plate the tuna steaks and top with teriyaki sauce.
  6. For serving, you can either serve the volcano sauce on the side for dipping or pipe it onto the tuna steaks using an airtight plastic bag with a small hole in one corner.
  7. Garnish with sliced scallions before serving.
  8. Enjoy your Teriyaki Tuna with Volcano Sauce!
Prep Time
10 minutes

Cooking Time
           
                 10 minutes

Total Time
