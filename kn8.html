Shrimp Fried Rice

Description:

Shrimp Fried Rice, is also something that I often make for breakfast or lunch when I have tiny shrimp available.

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups cooked white rice
  • ¾ cup frozen green peas
  • ¾ cup frozen small shrimp with no shell
  • ½ cup carrot, diced
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon onion powder
  • 3 tablespoons butter

Procedures:

  1. Combine the rice, salt, garlic powder, and onion powder. Gently mix to blend all the ingredients.
  2. Melt the butter in a pan. Once the butter starts to get hot (make sure not to get the butter very hot), put-in green peas and carrots. Cook for 1 to 2 minutes.
  3. Put the shrimp in the wok and cook for 1 minute.
  4. Add the rice mixture. Cook for 10 minutes over medium heat while tossing once in a while. Make sure to toss the ingredients while cooking to blend them properly.
  5. Transfer to a serving bowl. Serve for breakfast or lunch with your favorite dish. Share and enjoy!
