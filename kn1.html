Sisig Paella


Description:

Pinoy cooks really just love playing with rice, which likely stems from the natural love we have for this handy ingredient. This is why we have adapted plenty of great standalone rice dishes, such as paella.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb. pork belly grilled and diced
  • 1 Knorr Pork Cube
  • 1 chorizo de Bilbao diced
  • 5 quail eggs
  • 2 cups paella rice
  • 3 teaspoons Knorr Liquid Seasoning
  • 3 ½ cups water
  • ¼ cups green onions chopped
  • 2 teaspoons smoked paprika
  • 2 long green pepper sliced
  • ¼ cup red bell pepper chopped
  • 1 onion minced
  • 4 cloves garlic minced
  • 3 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 1 lemon wedged

Procedures:

  1. Heat oil. Sauté onion and garlic until the onion softens. Add chorizo, pork belly, and Knorr Pork Cube. Sauté for 1 minute. Add the paella rice. Cook for 1 minute while stirring. Pour the water into the pan and add Knorr Liquid Seasoning and bell pepper. Stir. Cover the pot. Continue cooking until the rice is done. Sprinkle green onions on top. Crack the eggs and top over the rice. Add long green pepper. Cover and cook for 2 minutes. Combine the dressing ingredients and drizzle over the sisig paella. Serve with lemon wedges. Share and enjoy!
