Hotdog Fried Rice Recipe


Description:

Hotdog Fried Rice is a delicious and satisfying dish that combines the flavors of savory hotdogs, fluffy rice, and aromatic seasonings. It's a quick and easy meal that's perfect for busy days or when you're craving a comforting homemade dish with a twist

Ingredients:

  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • 2 pcs hotdog, chopped into squares
  • 1 pc carrot, chopped into small pieces
  • Â½ cup green peas
  • 2 pc egg, beaten
  • 1 pc Knorr Chicken Broth Cube
  • 4 cup rice cooked

Procedures:

  1. Let's start by preparing the rice toppings. In medium pot, fry the chopped hotdogs, green peas, and carrots until soft and tender. Mix in the egg and continue frying until it sets. Occasionally stir the egg to make sure that all sides cook evenly. Add the rice into the pan. Remove all rice lumps. Add in the crushed Knorr Chicken Cubes and mix properly with the other ingredients.
