Ginisang Repolyo

—Recipe—

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cabbage, chopped
  • 1 large carrot, chopped
  • 300 g large shrimps, peeled and deveined
  • 1 cup chicken stock
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 medium white onion, thinly sliced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • Fish sauce
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Oil

Procedure

  1. Prepare a wok, turn heat to really high and once its smoking add oil then quickly stir fry shrimps for 30 seconds. Remove from wok then set aside.
  2. Saut√© garlic and onions then add the carrots, continue to stir fry for 30 seconds.
  3. Add the cabbage then stir fry for 30 seconds.
  4. Pour chicken stock and oyster sauce, continue to stir fry until vegetables are wilted but still crispy and the liquid is reduced.
  5. Add the shrimps back then season with fish sauce and freshly ground black pepper. Serve while hot.
