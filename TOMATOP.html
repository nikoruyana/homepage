Tomato squid stir fry

Tomato squid stir fry is really suitable for busy and quick lunch or dinner. The sauce is so tasty and sweet-sour savory!

Ingredients

  • 1 kg Large Squid
  • 5 Large Shallot chopped
  • 2 cloves Garlic finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp Tomato Paste
  • 2 tbsp Cooking Oil
  • 1/2 cup Water
  • Salt & Black Pepper
  • Chopped Green Onion for garnish
  • 1/2 tbsp Cornstarch
  • 3 tbsp Water (for cornstarch)

    • For Sauce

  • 3 Lemongrass Crushed
  • 3 tbsp Chili Sauce
  • 2 tbsp Oyster Sauce
  • 1,5 tsp Worcestershire Sauce
  • 1 tbsp Granulated Sugar
  • 1 tsp Chicken Stock Powder
  • 2 Chopped Bird’s Eye Chilis (optional)
  • 2 Bay Leaves (optional)

Instructions

  1. Cut the squid so the meat shaped like the rings. Cut in 1/3-1/2 inch thickness. You can discard the skin and the tentacles (optional).
  2. In large pan heat the cooking oil. Sauté the shallot for 1 minute then add garlic and continue cooking for 1 minute. Add tomato paste and stir for 2 minutes.
  3. Add chopped squid and water. The add all sauce ingredients. Continue cooking for 5 minutes. Adjust the taste with salt and black pepper.
  4. In small bowl combine cornstarch and 3 tbsp water. Add to the pan and stir until the sauce thicken.
  5. Turn off the heat and tomato squid stir fry is ready to serve.
Prep Time
10 minutes

Cooking Time
           
                 20 minutes

Total Time
           
                  30 minutes
