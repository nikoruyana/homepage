Camaron Rebosado

Camaron rebosado is a deep-fried battered shrimp dish in Philippine cuisine. It is usually served with a sweet and sour sauce. It is a common dish in Philippine cuisine.

Garlic Butter Shrimp

Garlic buttered shrimp is a popular dish in Filipino cuisine that features succulent shrimp cooked in a rich and flavorful sauce made from garlic and butter. The dish typically starts by sautéing minced garlic in butter until aromatic and golden brown.

Sinigang na Hipon

Sinigang na Hipon is a type of Filipino sour soup, wherein shrimp is used as the main ingredient. This dish also includes a variety of vegetables such as daikon radish, snake beans, okra, and eggplant. This dish is best served during cold weather. It is best enjoyed hot with a cup of white rice.

Sweet and Spicy

This recipe features fresh shrimp combined with sweet and spicy sauce or a wonderful stir-fried seafood dish that is perfect any night of the week. For a special Filipino dish, serve this dish with Sinangag rice.

Halabos na Hipon

It's a simple yet flavorful dish where fresh shrimp are steamed and typically served with a dipping sauce made of vinegar, soy sauce, and garlic. The shrimp are often cooked in their shells, which helps retain their natural flavors and juiciness. It's a popular and easy-to-prepare seafood dish enjoyed in Filipino cuisine.

Bicol Express

Seafood Bicol Express is a dish composed of fresh shrimp, squid, and mussels. These are cooked in coconut milk along with sautéed garlic, ginger, and onion. The addition of lots of chili peppers and shrimp paste made this dish extra spicy, tasty, and special.

Ginataang Hipon

Ginataang Hipon is a Filipino dish featuring shrimp cooked in a creamy coconut milk sauce flavored with garlic, onions, ginger, and sometimes chili peppers for a hint of spice.

Adobong Hipon

Adobong Hipon is a Filipino dish where shrimp is marinated and cooked in a savory sauce made from vinegar, soy sauce, garlic, and spices, creating a tangy and flavorful dish.

Curry na Hipon

Curry na Hipon is a Filipino dish featuring shrimp cooked in a flavorful curry sauce made with coconut milk, curry powder, onions, garlic, and spices, resulting in a creamy and aromatic dish.

Sipo egg with Shrimp

Sipo Egg with Shrimp is a Filipino dish consisting of hard-boiled eggs stuffed with a mixture of ground shrimp, vegetables like carrots and peas, and a creamy sauce typically made with mayonnaise and cheese.
