FRIED LAPU-LAPU

A whole deep-fried fish is one of life’s great culinary treats. Seriously. A stunningly fresh specimen of lapu-lapu or grouper, seasoned simply with salt then carefully lowered into very hot lard and fried just long enough to get a crisp skin while keeping the meat still very moist.

GINATAANG LAPU-LAPU

Ginataang Lapu Lapu is a Filipino dish prepared with grouper fish, moringa leaves and buttercup squash cooked in a chilli spiced coconut cream sauce.

SWEET AND SOUR LAPU-LAPU

Another thing about sweet and sour lapu-lapu is how a simple sauce of sweet and sour can make a regular fried fish extra special. Basically you can use any kind of fish. However, we regularly use the pink lapu-lapu, gray lapu-lapu and tilapia.

STEAMED LAPU-LAPU

If there is a party or fiesta food that resonates the most, it is steamed lapu-lapu (grouper) that always comes to mind.this lapu-lapu dish is so rich that it’s deviously good. It is healthy because it’s fish and vegetables.

STEAMED LAPU-LAPU WITH MAYONNAISE

Steamed Lapu-Lapu with Mayonnaise is a traditional Christmas dish made with steamed grouper fish topped with mayonnaise and garnished with chopped eggs, sweet pickle relish, carrots, and bell peppers.

ESCABECHENG LAPU-LAPU

Escabeche Lapu Lapu is easy to make for family dinners yet fancy enough for special occasions. This Filipino-style sweet and sour fish with pickled vegetables and sweet and tangy sauce is delicious with steamed rice and sure to be a crowd favorite.

SINIGANG NA LAPU-LAPU SA MISO

Sinigang na Lapu lapu sa miso is a Filipino sinigang version that makes use of grouper fish. Just like any other sinigang dishes, this is simple and easy to cook.

GRILLED LAPU-LAPU WITH SPICY GARLIC MAYO

Give this grilled fish dish a new twist using Lady's Choice Real Mayonnaise. Serve with our signature spicy mayo garlic and enjoy newfound taste!

LAPU-LAPU IN TAUSI SAUCE

This recipe has just the right contrast of freshness from the fish and saltiness from the black beans. It’s so delicious and an absolute crowd pleaser. Serve this as a focal dish in any special gatherings and you’ll surely impress all your guests.
