Ckicken Fried Rice

Description:

Filipinos love this side dish, and so sometimes, we underestimate just how easy it is to get full from one cup. Of course, you can always refrigerate your rice, and heat it up again the next day for your meals.

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups cooked white rice
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 6 ounces chicken breast, boneless
  • 1 ½ cups frozen mixed vegetables (carrots, green peas, and corn)
  • 1 onion, minced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 tablespoons cooking oil
  • Salt and ground black pepper to taste

Sauce Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon sesame oil

procedures:

  1. Cut the chicken into small pieces. Arrange in a bowl and combine with the sauce ingredients. Mix well. Set aside.
  2. Heat the oil in a pan. Fry the beaten eggs until done. Remove the cooked eggs from the pan. Chop them into pieces, and then set them aside.
  3. Using the remaining oil (add more if needed), sauté the garlic and onion.
  4. Add the marinated chicken once the onion softens. Stir-fry for 2 to 3 minutes.
  5. Add the vegetables. Continue stir-frying for 2 minutes.
  6. Add the rice. Toss until all the ingredients are well blended. Continue cooking for 3 minutes.
  7. Season with salt and ground black pepper.
  8. Transfer to a serving plate. Serve immediately.
