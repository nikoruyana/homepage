KINAMATISANG BISUGO

Looking for a special fish recipe for an occasion? This Special Kinamatisang Bisugo Soup is tasty, warm, comforting, and easy to make.

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp olive oil (for that extra special taste)
  • 45 grams bacon sliced and finely chopped
  • 1 cup white onion chopped
  • 1 pc green bell pepper diced
  • 1 celery diced
  • 1 can whole peeled tomatoes chopped “Molinera”
  • 800 ml fish stock (1 liter is 1000 ml)
  • 1 head Bisugo cleaned
  • 2 potatoes diced (2-3 cups)
  • salt to taste
  • Black Pepper (ground) to taste
  • 1/2 kilo Bisugo with head on fillet
  • 1/4 kilo shrimps
  • 1/4 kilo clams pre cooked

Instructions

  1. Cook the bacon in oil until tender but do not brown
  2. Add onions, bell pepper and celery. Saute for 2 minutes.
  3. Add tomatoes, fish stock, fish head, and bring to a boil.
  4. Add potatoes and simmer in medium heat till potatoes are tender.
  5. Take out the fish head.
  6. Season with salt and pepper.
  7. Then add the seafood (clams, shrimp, and bisugo) until cooked.

Prep Time
10 minutes

Cooking Time
           
                 8 minutes

Total Time
           
                 18 minutes
