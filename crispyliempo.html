Crispy Liempo

Filipino Cuisine

Prep Time : 15minutes

Cooking Time : 2hours

Crispy Liempo offers a delectable and crunchy pork belly option suitable for both lunch and dinner. It's a versatile dish that can be served on any occasion, providing a delightful alternative to lechon kawali. Panlasang Pinoy

Ingredients

  • 3 lbs Pork belly
  • 1 Â½ tablespoons salt
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 8 cloves garlic crushed

Instructions

  1. Arrange pork belly slab in a steamer. Steam for 1 hour. Remove from the steamer and let it cool down.
  2. Poke the skin using a sharp tool such as a metal skewer or a meat tenderizer with a sharp needle blade. Rub salt all over the pork. Let it stay for 10 minutes.
  3. Combine olive oil and garlic. Rub all over the pork belly. Let it stay for at least 1 hour. Note: I usually marinate the pork overnight for best results.
  4. Remove the garlic from the pork. Arrange inside a turbo broiler. Broil in 365F for 45 minutes or until crispy.
