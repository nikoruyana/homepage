Inihaw na Pusit

Stuffed with bits of tomato and sweet onion, then grilled to perfection to create a tender but smoky taste, this recipe is definitely one for the books. This is probably why you can count on this dish popping up during family outings, or summer gatherings whenever a grill is at your disposal.

Ingredients

  • 3 squid cleaned
  • 1 cup soy sauce
  • 1 tomato diced
  • 1 yellow onion diced
  • 3 pieces Thai chili
  • 1/2 lemon
  • Salt and ground black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large container, combine onion and tomato
  2. 1 tomato, 1 yellow onion
  3. Add some salt and pepper and mix well.
  4. Salt and ground black pepper to taste
  5. Stuff the squid with the onion and tomato mixture. Add more if necessary. 3 squid
  6. Grill the squid for 6 to 8 minutes per side.
  7. Transfer to a serving plate.
  8. Make the dipping sauce by combining soy sauce, chili pepper, and lemon. Stir.
  9. 1 cup soy sauce, 3 pieces Thai chili, 1/2 lemon
Cooking Time
40 minutes
Serving
3 people
