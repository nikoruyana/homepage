Paksiw na Tulingan

Paksiw na Tulingan is a must-try Filipino dish, boasting the flavor of the sea. It’s a traditional Filipino dish made of Tulingan, which translates to the English word “mackerel tuna.”However, Tulingan or mackerel tuna is an oily fish with a strong flavor. Thus, cooking it in vinegar, ginger, and garlic is ideal to reduce its oily and fishy taste.

Sinaing na Tulingan

Sinaing na Tulingan is a Filipino Fish Recipe that translates to braised fish. The recipe makes use of bullet tuna. It is a type of saltwater fish locally known in the Philippines as “tulingan”. This dish is famous in the coastal towns of Batangas.

Sinigang na Tulingan

This sinigang is a healthy and delicious soup that’s full of good-for-you vegetables like mustasa. The tulingan also boosts a healthy dose of Omega 3 acids that make this dish not only delicious but also good for you.

Tinolang Tulingan

Tinolang Tulingan is an easy, one-pot soup from the Philippines. However, instead of chicken, you can use tulingan as the main ingredient with moringa leaves and bell pepper, simmered with fresh ginger in a savory broth.

Tortang Tulingan

Tortang Tuna with Spinach is a type of omelet wherein sautéed tuna and spinach are added to the beaten egg mixture before cooking.

Tuna Sarciado

Tuna In Can Sarciado is done by simmering canned tuna in a sauce that is usually composed of tomatoes and onions.

Adobong Tulingan

Adobong Tulingan is a Filipino dish that made use of tulingan stewed in a marinade of vinegar, soy sauce, garlic, herbs, and spices.

Ginataang Tulingan

Ginataang Tulingan is a Filipino dish wherein fish is cooked in coconut milk along with eggplant and other ingredients. The fish used in this recipe is locally referred to as tulingan. It is also known as bullet tuna or bonito.

Ginisang Tulingan

This is a simple Ginisang Tuna Recipe that will give you a delicious fresh tasting dish that can be topped with mixed greens and eaten as a salad; it can also be mixed with mayonnaise and enjoyed like a tuna salad sandwich.

Inihaw na Tulingan

This Inihaw na isda uses “tulingan,” a local tuna variety. There are numerous ways to prepare inihaw na isda tulingan, or grilled fish; however, following this recipe will show you how to do it simply.
