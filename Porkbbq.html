Pork BBQ

Filipino Cuisine

Prep Time : 3 hours

Cooking Time : 15minutes

Filipino Style Pork BBQ is characterized by pork barbecue cooked on bamboo skewers. This dish consists of thinly sliced pork marinated in a unique blend of seasonings and spices. In the Philippines, it's typically grilled over wood charcoal and served with a side of spicy vinegar for dipping.

Marinade Ingredients

  • 4 lbs. pork shoulder
  • ¾ cup soy sauce
  • ½ cup juice extracted from calamansi or lemon
  • ¾ cup banana ketchup
  • 4 tablespoons dark brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 ½ cups lemon or lime soda

Instructions

  1. Arrange the pork slices in a large bowl.
  2. Once the oil becomes hot, saute onion and garlic.
  3. Add pork belly. Continue to saute until it turns light brown.
  4. Add Chorizo de Bilbao. Cook for 1 minute.
  5. Add all the marinade ingredients. Mix well. Cover the bowl and marinate for at least 3 hours.
  6. Fasten the marinated pork together using bamboo skewers.
  7. Heat-up the grill. Start to grill your Filipino Style pork BBQ for 3 to 5 minutes per side until the meat is thoroughly cooked.
  8. Put the baby bok choy into the pot. Cover. Cook for 2 minutes.
  9. Make sure to always baste the BBQ when flipping it over. Note: the basting sauce ingredients are the same as the marinade ingredients minus the lemon soda.
