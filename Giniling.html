Giniling

Filipino Cuisine

Prep Time : 15 minutes

Cooking Time : 1 hour

Looking for a simple yet delicious lunch idea? Why not try making Pork Giniling with Quail Eggs? It's a delightful twist on the classic pork giniling dish, made more appealing to kids with the addition of quail eggs. Children often enjoy cute and fun elements in their meals, and the quail eggs add that touch. If you're struggling to get your kids to eat their meals, this dish might just do the trick.

  • 2 lbs ground pork

  • 1 8 oz. can tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup raisins
