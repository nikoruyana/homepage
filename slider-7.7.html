SINANGLAY HASA-HASA

Cooking sinanglay na hasa-hasa requires wrapping fish in a leafy vegetable, like pechay or bok choy, and slowly stewing it in coconut milk. This fish dish is quick and easy to prepare, making it the perfect recipe for those on the go.

HASA-HASA FILLET

Hasa-Hasa fillet refers to the flesh of the Hasa-Hasa fish that has been deboned and cut into fillets. These fillets are often used in various seafood dishes for their mild flavor and firm texture.

GRILLED HASA-HASA

Grilled hasa-hasa, a staple in Filipino cuisine, is a dish that embodies simplicity and flavor. The hasa-hasa, or short-bodied mackerel, is known for its delicate yet flavorful flesh, making it an excellent choice for grilling.

SINABAWANG HASA-HASA

Sinabawang Isda with Malunggay is a comforting and nutritious Filipino fish soup that combines the delicate flavors of hasa-hasa (short-bodied mackerel) with the health benefits of malunggay (moringa) leaves. This dish is not only delicious but also packed with vitamins and minerals, making it a popular choice for family meals, especially during cooler weather or when someone needs a nourishing pick-me-up.
