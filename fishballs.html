Fish Balls

description

Fish balls are traditionally sold in mobile wooden carts by street vendors who peddle around the streets. The balls are deep fried in hot oil until they grow bigger like a mini balloon and turn golden.

Ingredients

  • 300 grams Fish fillet sole or cream dory, chopped (boiled in water with little salt then drained)
  • ½ cup fish water from boiled water
  • 1 ½ cup MAYA All-purpose Flour
  • ¼ cup MAYA Cornstarch
  • 2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon garlic minced
  • 1 piece medium-sized onion chopped
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 ½ teaspoon black pepper
  • ¼ cup spring onions chopped
  • 2 egg whites
  • extra oil for frying

Procedure

  1. Mix all ingredients in a food processor and process until fully incorporated, smooth and sticky.
  2. Transfer mixture in a bowl then cover with plastic wrap and set aside to rest for about 15 minutes.
  3. Scoop fish ball mixture into your lightly oiled palm and shape mixture into a ball. Do the same procedure with the rest of the mixture.
  4. Deep fry in hot oil. Best serve with brown dipping sauce or sweet and sour sauce.
