Ginataang Galunggong

Ginataang Galunggong is a Filipino fish stew that uses galunggong or round scad as the main ingredient cooked in coconut milk!

Inihaw na Galunggong is a simple meal composed of grilled fish, along with rice and a spicy vinegar dip.

Paksiw na Galunggong is a Filipino dish wherein fish such as galunggong is cooked with water, vinegar, and some spices.

Sinigang Galunggong

The sinigang na isda is a Pinoy stew made with tender galunggong with kangkong, gabi, beans, radish and okra in a sauce made by ginger, onion. tomatoes and chilies.

Smoked Galunggong

Tinapa is a Filipino culinary term to define smoked fish, it is a popular delicacy which is usually made out of milkfish or galunggong (mackerel scad), usually sold in wet markets

Adobong Galunggong

Adobong Galunggong is a Filipino fish dish, made with the iconic adobo sauce reinvented to suit the galunggong with a bit of a heat and intense flavor.
